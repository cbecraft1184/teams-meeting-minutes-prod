# NAVY ERP Meeting Minutes Demo - Executive Summary

## Overview

This is a 4-6 week proof-of-concept demonstration for 20 NAVY ERP personnel using the ABC123987.onmicrosoft.com tenant. The system automatically captures Microsoft Teams meetings, generates AI-powered meeting minutes using Azure OpenAI (GPT-4o), manages review and approval workflow, and archives to SharePoint. The demo validates whether AI automation can save 30-60 minutes per meeting while improving decision tracking and knowledge management for NAVY ERP operations.

Success criteria include 100% meeting capture rate, 80%+ AI accuracy requiring minimal editing, and 8/10 user satisfaction. If the pilot proves value, production deployment would require a separate 6-12 month effort including ATO process, GCC High migration, and IL4/IL5 compliance assessment. At the end of Week 6, leadership will make a go/no-go decision based on demonstrated time savings, user adoption, and operational impact metrics.
